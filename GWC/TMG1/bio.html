About Girls Who Code

The time management website you are viewing was created by the Girls Who Code club at North Central College. After 20 weeks of learning to code, we have mastered several skills by working with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This project was a great exposure to the world of computer science and being part of a this club inspires girls all around the world to pursue working in a field of technology. The Girls Who Code club in Naperville focuses on not just teaching girls to code, but also on educating them to build powerful friendships with other girls and coming together to make a community impact using coding. We hope you enjoy using our project and hope it comes to full use
